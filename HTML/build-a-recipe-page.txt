    <h1>Sinigang</h1> 
    <p>a classic Filipino soup known for its sour and savory flavor, often made with tamarind as the souring agent. It's a popular comfort food, usually served with rice. Sinigang can be made with various meats like pork, beef, fish, or chicken, and a mix of vegetables like string beans, okra, and bok choy, among others. </p>

      <li>1 eggplant (talong), sliced</li>

      <li>1–2 long green chili peppers (siling haba)</li>
      <li>1 tablespoon fish sauce (patis)</li>
      <li>Salt and pepper, to taste</li>
      <li>6–8 cups water</li>
